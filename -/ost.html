짜 OSt - A Context Aware Music Player

This project aims to be the soundtrack to your OS - to make navigating your desktop environment feel like an adventure through a virtual world.

Simply put, when you change windows, OSt changes what you're listening to. It allows you to pair audio tracks to the processes running on your computer, then when switching to a new window/program/process OSt will seamlessly transition to the track that is paired to the new process.

짜 Features

  • Assign multiple tracks to individual processes.
  • Variety of playback modes:
  • Loop - loop the track until proceses switch with configurable loop start/end points.
  • Variety - randomly play tracks assigned to process but don't repeat tracks until all have played.
  • Random - randomly play tracks assinged to process with repeats possible.
  • Multi-Platform - currently supported in Windows 7+ and OSX
  • Extensibility
  • The primary OSt component runs as a small http server on your computer allowing for a variety of front-ends to monitor your context. Possible future front-ends include:
  • Changing tracks based on what tab you have open in your IDE.
  • Changing tracks based on what tab you have focused in your web browser.
